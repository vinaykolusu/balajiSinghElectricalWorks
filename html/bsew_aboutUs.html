Welcome to Balaji Singh Electrical Works (BSEW), a six decade old establishment, an epitome to service industry of motors, generators and other industrial machinery in the country. We strive to work towards helping our clients outperform by offering frugal services with nonpareil quality and shortest delivery time. Our unbelievably long-standing customer relations and ~100% customer retention is a testament to the highest order of integrity and candour that we maintain with our business partners.

BSEW owes its genesis, eminence and acclaim to the visionary and technocrat-entrepreneur Late Sri. Balaji Singh Bidesi. After completing his training from Military College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineers, Secunderabad and serving Nizam State Power House, he founded the company in the year 1958 in Secunderabad, Andhra Pradesh. Since its inception, the company has grown to earn innumerable customer accolades for its service quality, reliability and fair pricing.

Sri. Ramesh Singh Bidesi, the current Proprietor of BSEW, after completing his M.Tech in Electrical Engineering from IIT Madrad in the year 1983 has steered the business from a modest beginning of a 200 sqft facility to a multi storied 3500 sqft state of the art facility for servicing motors and generators of all kinds. This unmatched progress is a testimony to his exceptional technical skills resulting from a perfect blend of practical as well as theoretical knowledge built over 30 years.
